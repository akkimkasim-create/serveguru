Our Story

Bharat Trading Corporation has been serving Coimbatore and surrounding regions as a trusted sewing machine dealer and service provider. Located in the heart of Singanallur, we have built our reputation on quality products, honest pricing, and exceptional after-sales service.

From domestic sewing machines for home use to heavy-duty industrial machines for garment factories, we have the perfect machine for every need. As authorized dealers for 9+ premium brands, we offer the widest selection in the region.
